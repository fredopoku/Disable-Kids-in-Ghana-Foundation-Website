Who We Are

Born From Experience.
Driven by Love.

AniGrace Foundation was founded in 2026 by a mother who knows — not from a textbook, but from daily life — how overwhelming it is to care for a person with a disability without the right resources. She built what she wished had existed.

We are based in the United Kingdom and Ghana, but our reach goes wherever the need is greatest — across Africa and beyond. We focus especially on communities where formal support systems are absent or inaccessible.

Support is not just financial. We donate wheelchairs, prosthetics, hearing aids, walking frames, adaptive tools, and more — because sometimes what changes a life is not money but the right piece of equipment in the right hands.

What We Do

How We Show Up

We go beyond donations. AniGrace supports people with disabilities through equipment, education, care, and community — in whatever form is most needed.

Equipment & Assistive Devices

Wheelchairs, walking frames, hearing aids, prosthetic limbs, white canes, adaptive feeding tools — we source, fund, and deliver the equipment that restores independence and dignity to people who otherwise cannot access it.

  • Wheelchairs & mobility aids
  • Hearing & vision aids
  • Prosthetics & orthotics
  • Adaptive daily-living tools

Education & Inclusion

We work with schools, families, and communities to ensure children and young people with disabilities are not left behind in education. From inclusive classroom resources to teacher training, we build environments where every learner belongs.

  • Inclusive learning resources
  • Teacher awareness workshops
  • School accessibility support

Care & Family Support

Caregivers need support too. We provide guidance, peer networks, and practical resources for parents and family members navigating the healthcare and support system — especially those doing it alone and far from home.

  • Caregiver peer networks
  • Navigating care systems
  • Mental health signposting

Pan-African Outreach

We reach communities across Africa where disability support is virtually non-existent. Through partners on the ground and our own visits, we deliver aid in person to people who have no other avenue for help.

  • Rural community missions
  • Partner NGO collaboration
  • Diaspora-led aid delivery

Skills & Livelihoods

For young people and adults with disabilities, we support pathways to independence through vocational training, entrepreneurship mentoring, and connections to employment opportunities that embrace difference.

  • Vocational training
  • Entrepreneurship grants
  • Job-readiness coaching

Advocacy & Awareness

We raise the conversation around disability in Africa — challenging stigma, educating communities, and advocating for policy changes that protect and uplift people with disabilities and their families.

  • Community workshops
  • Social media campaigns
  • Policy engagement

Real Lives

Stories of Change

Every piece of equipment, every visit, every phone call — it means the world to the person on the other end.

Equipment

A Wheelchair That Changed Everything

Northern Ghana

A young boy had never left his family's compound because they had no way to move him. AniGrace delivered a wheelchair. Within a week, he was at school for the first time in his life.

Care Support

A Mother Finally Heard

Accra, Ghana

Struggling alone for years with a non-verbal child, one mother connected with AniGrace's caregiver network. Communication aids, peer support, and finally — someone who understood exactly what she was going through.

Livelihoods

From Barriers to Business

Kumasi, Ghana

Told she would never be employed because of her limb difference, she joined our skills programme. Today she runs her own tailoring business and has trained two other young women with disabilities.
